# Gamerz Lounge — Music as a Game

> Part of the MoyaCode **Game Launch** series (Gamerz Lounge).
> Conceived by the founder, 2026-06-29.

---

## The core idea

**Music becomes a game.** Every musical **artist is a character** in the game world.

You are **joining the music world**. Your power is **knowing the lyrics** of an artist — and that knowledge is what **opens doors to new worlds**.

## Worlds = musical journeys

Each musical **journey / genre is its own world**:
- **Afrobeat world** (the Afrobeat journey) — first example.
- Other genres/journeys become other worlds.

To enter and succeed in a world, **your artist (character) must become familiar with that environment** — you have to "fit" the world to thrive in it. Mastering an artist's lyrics is how you level up and unlock the next door.

## Structure

- **Characters** = the artists themselves.
- **Worlds** = genres / musical journeys (Afrobeat, etc.).
- **Stages / levels** within each world.
- **Progression:** know the lyrics → prove familiarity → unlock the next door / world.

## Why this connects to everything

- It's the **genesis** of the broader "MoyaCode game becomes a WORLD" vision — featuring the community's **music** (including the founder's own), **art** (the character submissions we collect), and **storylines**.
- The **lyrics-as-knowledge** mechanic is the same RAG-over-lyrics tech as **No Noise Studio** (`desktop/no-noise-studio`) — one feeds learning, the other feeds play.
- Lives under the **Gamerz Lounge** Game Launch banner (see `gamerz_lounge_poster.png` in this folder).
