*Ajasin Foundation — Paystack Account Activation*

Documents needed to activate our Paystack account so the Foundation can receive donations online.

Since the Foundation is registered with the CAC as *Incorporated Trustees*, Paystack requires the following:

*1) Organisation documents*
• CAC Certificate of Incorporation (Incorporated Trustees)
• CAC/IT registration number
• Form CAC IT/1 (the Incorporated Trustees application form)
• TIN (Tax Identification Number) for the Foundation
• Proof of address: a recent utility bill (electricity, water, waste, or cable TV), not older than 6 months, showing the Foundation's name and address
• A corporate bank account in the Foundation's name (this is where Paystack settles the money)
• SCUML certificate (Paystack may request this for NGOs)

*2) For at least TWO trustees / directors (each person provides)*
• A government-issued ID: NIN slip, Driver's Licence, Voter's Card, or International Passport
• Their BVN (Bank Verification Number)
• A personal bank account number

*Very important:* the names and details must match EXACTLY across all the documents.

The documents are uploaded in the Paystack Dashboard, under Settings → Compliance.

Once this is done, our website's online donations go live.
