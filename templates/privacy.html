Privacy

Privacy Policy

MoyaCode teaches young people to code. We built it for students, and we take their privacy seriously.

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Who this is for

MoyaCode is designed for Nigerian secondary-school students (JSS1–SS3), who are usually minors. Because of that, we collect as little personal information as possible and we design every feature — including our AI assistant — to be safe for children.

What we collect

  • On your device (default): your learning progress — which lessons you've done, XP, streaks, and your onboarding choices — is stored in your browser's local storage. It stays on your device and is not sent to us unless you create an account.
  • If you create an account: we store a login identifier and the same learning-progress data in a secure database so you can continue on another device. Each account can only ever see its own data (enforced by per-user database rules).
  • If you submit a character or game: we store what you type in the submission form so we can review and feature it. Please don't include personal details you wouldn't want shared.

We do not ask for or need your home address, and we ask you never to share passwords, phone numbers, or precise location.

Our AI assistant (MoyaBot)

MoyaBot is an AI assistant, not a person. It helps you learn to code and find your way around MoyaCode. To power MoyaBot's chat, the messages you send are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI service under their terms. MoyaBot is instructed never to ask for your personal information, and we do not use your chats to build a profile of you. Please keep personal details out of the chat.

How we use information

  • To show your progress and recommend what to learn next.
  • To review and feature student submissions (with review before anything is shown publicly).
  • To keep the service safe and working.

We do not sell your information or use it for advertising.

Your rights and choices

You can clear the data stored on your device at any time by clearing your browser storage. If you have an account and want your data removed, or if a parent or teacher has a question, use the contact path below. We follow the principles of Nigeria's Data Protection Act (NDPA 2023) and child-privacy standards such as COPPA.

Parents & teachers: MoyaCode is meant to be used with your guidance. See our Child-Safety page for how we protect students and how to report a concern.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Use the "Report a problem" option in the MoyaBot assistant, or have a parent or teacher reach out to the MoyaCode team through your school coordinator.
