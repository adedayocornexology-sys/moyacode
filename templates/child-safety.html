Child Safety

Keeping students safe

MoyaCode is built for children. Safety isn't an add-on — it shapes how the whole product, including our AI assistant, is designed.

Last updated: 20 July 2026

You're talking to an AI

Our assistant, MoyaBot, is an artificial-intelligence system, not a human. We always tell students this clearly in the chat. MoyaBot's only job is to help you learn to code and use MoyaCode.

How we protect students

  • Age-appropriate by design. MoyaBot is instructed to keep every reply kind, safe, and on the topic of learning to code, and to decline anything unsafe or off-topic.
  • No personal information. MoyaBot never asks for a student's full name, address, school, phone number, passwords, location, or photos — and reminds students to keep those private.
  • Reviewed submissions. Anything students submit (characters, games) is reviewed by a person before it can appear publicly.
  • Data minimisation. We collect as little as possible and never sell it. See our Privacy Policy.
  • Safety technology. MoyaBot runs on Anthropic's Claude with additional child-safety instructions and content limits layered on top.

For parents and teachers

MoyaCode works best alongside a caring adult. We encourage you to sit with your student sometimes, talk about what they're building, and remind them of the online-safety basics: keep personal details private, and tell a trusted adult if anything feels wrong.

Our commitments

We follow Anthropic's guidelines for organisations serving minors and applicable child-protection standards, including Nigeria's Data Protection Act (NDPA 2023) and the principles of COPPA. We keep these safeguards under review and improve them over time.

Report a problem

If MoyaBot ever says something inappropriate, or if you see anything unsafe on MoyaCode, please tell us. A parent or teacher can help.

  • Tell a trusted adult (parent, guardian, or teacher) right away.
  • Have them contact the MoyaCode team through your school coordinator, describing what happened and when.

We take every report seriously and will act quickly.
