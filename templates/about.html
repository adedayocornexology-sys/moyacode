Our Story

Coding shouldn't feel
out of reach.

MoyaCode is a game-style coding journey built in Owo, Nigeria, for students from JSS1 to SS3. We turn "coding is too hard" into "give me one more quest" — with XP, hearts, streaks, and lessons that actually make sense.

🎯

Why we exist

Too many brilliant students never touch code because the on-ramp is intimidating. We make the first step playful, free, and impossible to fail at.

🧠

How we teach

Scratch, HTML, CSS, JavaScript — and a growing Python track — delivered as bite-size quests with instant feedback, not walls of theory.

🌍

Who it's for

Secondary-school students across Africa, their teachers, and parents who want to see real progress they can track.

🎮

The Arcade

Our students don't just learn — they build. The best student-made games get featured in our Arcade for the world to play.
